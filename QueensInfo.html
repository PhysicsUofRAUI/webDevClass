Steps to Play With 4 Players

  • Two teams are formed, and the teamates sit across from each other
  • The current dealer takes a cut of the deck deal all players 13 cards. The person who deals switches everytime going around clockwise.
  • If the current dealer has a perfect cut, that person's team gets 100 points
  • Everyone gets a turn starting with the person to the right of the dealer
  • Goes around the table clockwise
  • Pick up a card from the middle deck or, if possible, the entire discard pile
  • The player can pick up discard pile if the player has a pair that matches the last card discarded
  • In the turn players lay down cards if:
    1. they have three cards that match
    2. two cards that match and a wild card
    3. cards that match cards they or their partner have already layed down
  • Then the player discards one card
  • This goes on till someone has no cards left
  • The team who goes out gets an extra 50 points
  • Each team counts the points left in their hands and the points they have laid down.
  • Then each team takes the points that was laid down and subtracts points that were in there hand, and this is their points for the turn.
  • These steps are continued till one team reaches a predetermined point level and goes out as well.
