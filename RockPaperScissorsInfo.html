How To Play

You play by using your fist to make a rock, a paper, or a scissors. Your oppenent does the same The winner is decided by taking the two entries from the players and comparing them to a set of pre-decided outcomes that are as follows:

  1. If both players make the same choice then it is a tie
  2. Rock beats Scissors
  3. Paper beats Rock
  4. Scissors beats Paper
