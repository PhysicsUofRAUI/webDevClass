Tic-Tac-Toe

Play!

How To Play

  • You play on a 3 by 3 grid, and there is two players.
  • Each player is either O's or X's
  • The players take turns placing their players (X's or O's) in one of the grid squares
  • This goes on until all the grid squares are filled or if one player gets three in a row
  • The winner is whoever gets three in a row first
  • If noone gets three in a row then it ends in a tie
  • Interesting note: If you start first there is a way you can always win
